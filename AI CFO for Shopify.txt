Primary Goal: Increase merchant profitability through AI-powered recommendations and financial intelligence.

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Vision

Build the first AI-powered Chief Financial Officer for Shopify merchants.

Instead of showing merchants more analytics dashboards, the app proactively identifies profit leaks, growth opportunities, inventory risks, and operational inefficiencies.

The app should answer:

"What should I do today to increase profit?"

rather than:

"Here are your numbers."
